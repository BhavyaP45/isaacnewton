Isaac Newton

Description

Isaac Newton is an English physicist and mathmatician that has contributed greatly to the field due to various discoveries.

Discoveries

  • In the field of optics, he discovered how white light is actually composed of an entire spectrum of light, from red to violet.
  • He created the three laws of motion, which are a key concept in modern day physics, which paved the path for the creation of the law of universal gravitation.
  • He is said to have been the original discoverer of calculus regarding very small numbers approaching zero.
  • His work Philosophiae Naturalis Principia Mathematica (Mathematical Principles of Natural Philosophy, 1687) is an important work for modern science.
